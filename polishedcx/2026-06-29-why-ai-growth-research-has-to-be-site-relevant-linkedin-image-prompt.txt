FULL-CONCEPT SYNTHESIS
Opening tension: AI growth advice often looks polished before it proves anything, creating a gap between confident recommendations and trustworthy strategy.
Argument journey: The article moves from generic website analysis to a stricter model: start with distinct buyer pressure, separate verified evidence from inference, connect product features to operational outcomes, and define which decisions should remain human-led.
Resolution / payoff: The reader walks away believing that useful AI research is narrower, more disciplined, and more honest about evidence, automation boundaries, and buyer-specific growth pressure.
Visual concept: A glossy but flimsy strategy machine is being forced through a strict evidence frame, revealing a clearer path from messy assumptions to grounded growth decisions.
